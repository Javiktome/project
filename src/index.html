The Campus of Karamalmi

Smart Solutions Developer Campus

A new Smart Solutions Developer Campus was opened in Karamalmi, Espoo, in August 2019. The ICT campus in Espoo has number one, not zero.

The campus has good connections to companies and the world. Here, the project is to create tomorrow’s solutions using smart technology and process management.

Access

The Karamalmi campus is located in Karamalmi, Espoo, at Karaportti 2.

  • To the east of the intersection of Turuntie and Ring Road II
  • Less than 4 kilometers west of Leppävaara
  • Just over a kilometer from Kera train station, a quarter of a walk north
  • Leppävaara has good bus connections to the campus, e.g. buses to the west 532, 224, 225, 227, 235, 236, 238, 239. From the center of Helsinki, trains A and U bring you to Leppävaara. See up-to-date train and bus route timetables on HSL's route and timetable service
  • The airport is about 22 km away: about 22 minutes by car.
